    The Team

    As an open soure project, much of our code has been initiated, created or improved by the community. We want to thank all our contributors and the open source community at large. Red Hat, as a company, is the catalyst in this communities of customers, contributors, and partners creating better technology the open source way.

    For many open source contributors recognition is a key driving factor. We encourage all contributors (active and inactive) to contact us (on the mailing list or privately) if you want your details changed or added. Beyond title headers this list is in no particular order.

    Michael Anstis

    • Drools Guvnor
    • IRC nickname: manstis

    I live in England and am married with two children. I cut my "programming" teeth writing various games commercially in the 1990's; before moving into a corporate environment after studying maths, physics and electronics at college. I became an Associate of the Chartered Insurance Institute during a previous career in insurance. I found Drools when looking for a Rules Engine to support the migration and consolidation of a legacy LISP application at Ford Motor Company. Excited by my discovery of the whole "rules" domain I hung around in the community aiding fellow adopters until now, being in the right place at the right time, I was fortunate enough to be offered a position on the core development team leading future development of Guvnor's guided editors. Working at Redhat marks a return to my guerrilla development days and escape from corporate bureaucracy into a world of freedom, opportunity and possibility.
